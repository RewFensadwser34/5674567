Server Rules

Follow these rules to ensure a fair and enjoyable experience

01
No Hacking or Cheating Permanent Ban

Use of any hacked clients, mods, or external tools that give unfair advantages is strictly prohibited. This includes but is not limited to: X-ray, auto-clickers, fly hacks, kill aura, and any form of exploitation.

02
Respect All Players Warning/Mute

Harassment, hate speech, discrimination, and excessive toxicity are not tolerated. Treat everyone with respect regardless of their background, skill level, or opinions.

03
No Spamming or Advertising Mute/Kick

Do not spam chat with repeated messages or excessive capitalization. Advertising other servers, Discord invites, or external links without staff permission is prohibited.

04
No Abusing Glitches Kick/Ban

Report any bugs or glitches to staff immediately. Intentionally using exploits, dupes, or game-breaking bugs for personal gain will result in punishment.

05
No Account Sharing Ban

Each player must use their own account. Sharing accounts, especially for the purpose of evading punishments or boosting stats, is strictly forbidden.

06
No Scamming or Real Money Trading Permanent Ban

Scamming other players in trades is not allowed. Trading in-game items for real money or items on other servers is strictly prohibited and will result in immediate action.

07
Follow Staff Instructions Kick/Ban

Staff members are here to help maintain a fair environment. Always follow their instructions. If you disagree with a decision, appeal through proper channels rather than arguing in-game.
